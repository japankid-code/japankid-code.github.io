イッシー, Issie

The name is formed in analogy with "Nessie" (the Loch Ness Monster).

  • Scientific Name:Issiteras rhombopteryx
  • Length:30 meters (98.4 feet)
  • Lifespan:???
  • Habitat:Lake Ikeda, Kyushu Island

According to mythology, Issie was a white mare turned into a lake monster due to the despair of losing her child to a samurai. Since then she frequently surfaces on the shore of Lake Ikeda, trying to find her lost child. Although, Issie may not be as alone as she thinks since it seems she has a cousin called Kusshi in Hokkaido, northern Japan.
